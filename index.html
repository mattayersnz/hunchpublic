Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install Hunch on my Mac?

Download the app from the Mac App Store, then follow the on-screen instructions to install it.

Can I sync Hunch across multiple devices?

Yes, Hunch supports synchronization across all your devices using your iCloud account.

How do I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password" to receive reset instructions via email.
